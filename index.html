Frequently Asked Questions

Is StayWise only for PGs?

No, StayWise is incredibly flexible. You can manage PGs, residential apartments, commercial offices, and shops all from the same dashboard effortlessly.

Can I manage multiple properties?

Yes! With our Growth and Pro plans, you can manage multiple properties and switch between them seamlessly within a single account.

Do you support cash + UPI tracking?

Absolutely. You can log payments via cash, UPI, bank transfer, or cheque. The ledger maintains a crystal-clear history of all transaction types.

Can managers have limited access?

Yes. StayWise features role-based access control. You can grant property managers access to only specific properties, and restrict their ability to edit past settled ledgers.

Is this invite-only now?

StayWise is currently operating as an invite-only MVP to ensure high-quality onboarding. You can request a demo and join our waitlist for instant priority access.
